##  Project Overview

This project analyzes one year of sales data for **Vrinda Store** using **Microsoft Excel** to uncover customer purchasing patterns, regional performance, and sales channel effectiveness.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify customer trends, and make data-driven business decisions.

The project follows a complete data analysis workflow, including:

- Data Cleaning
- Data Preparation
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Pivot Tables
- Interactive Dashboard Development
- Business Insight Generation

---

##  Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer purchasing behavior across different demographics, states, and sales channels to identify opportunities for improving sales and marketing strategies.

##  Analytical Questions

This project answers the following business questions:

##  Key Insights

###  Customer Demographics

- Women account for approximately **65%** of total purchases.
- Customers aged **30–49 years** contribute nearly **50%** of overall sales.

###  Regional Performance

The top three revenue-generating states are:

- Maharashtra
- Karnataka
- Uttar Pradesh

###  Sales Channels

The highest sales originate from:

- Amazon
- Flipkart
- Myntra

This indicates a strong customer preference for major e-commerce platforms.

---

##  Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve sales performance:

- Target marketing campaigns towards **women aged 30–49 years**.
- Increase promotional activities in **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh**, where sales are highest.
- Strengthen partnerships and promotional campaigns on **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra**.
- Offer personalized discounts and targeted campaigns for the highest-value customer segment.
- Allocate marketing budgets based on regional and channel performance to maximize return on investment (ROI).
